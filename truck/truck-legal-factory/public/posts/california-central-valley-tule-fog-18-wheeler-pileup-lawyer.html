Tactic 1: Delaying Payment

Insurance companies may delay payment to wear down the claimant or create a sense of urgency. Countermeasure: Document everything: Keep a detailed record of all correspondence with the insurance company, including dates, times, and contact information.

Tactic 2: Denying Liability

Insurance companies may deny liability to avoid paying out on a claim. Countermeasure: Gather evidence: Collect evidence to support your claim, including witness statements, police reports, and medical records.

Tactic 3: Undervaluing Claims

Insurance companies may undervalue claims to reduce the amount of compensation paid out. Countermeasure: Hire an expert appraiser: Hire an expert appraiser to evaluate the value of your claim and provide a detailed report.

Tactic 4: Using Pre-Existing Conditions

Tactic 5: Using Social Media

Countermeasure: Be cautious on social media: Be cautious about what you post on social media, and consider setting your accounts to private to avoid sharing information with insurance companies.
