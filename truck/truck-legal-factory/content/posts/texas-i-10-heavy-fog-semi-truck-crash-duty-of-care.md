2. **Denying Liability**: Insurance companies may deny liability for a claim, even if the evidence suggests that the defendant was at fault.
3. **Undervaluing Claims**: Insurance companies may undervalue claims, hoping that the victim will not notice the discrepancy.
4. **Using Delay Tactics**: Insurance companies may use delay tactics, such as requesting additional information or documentation, to slow down the claims process.
5. **Making Lowball Offers**: Insurance companies may make lowball offers, hoping that the victim will accept the offer and settle the claim for a lower amount.
