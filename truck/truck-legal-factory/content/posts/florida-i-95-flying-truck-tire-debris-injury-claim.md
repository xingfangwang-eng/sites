4. **Offering Lowball Settlements**: The insurance company may offer lowball settlements, hoping that the injured driver will accept the offer without fully understanding the value of her claim.
Countermeasure: Research the value of similar claims and be prepared to negotiate for a fair settlement.
5. **Denial of Medical Expenses**: The insurance company may deny medical expenses, claiming that they were not related to the accident.
Countermeasure: Gather detailed medical records and provide evidence to support the medical expenses, including witness statements and expert testimony.
