Insurance companies often employ various tactics to deny or reduce claims, including:

1. **Stalling and delay**: Insurance companies may drag out the claims process, making it difficult for victims to obtain the compensation they deserve.
2. **Denial of coverage**: Insurance companies may deny coverage for certain damages or losses, even if the policy provides coverage for those specific types of damages.
3. **Undervaluation of damages**: Insurance companies may undervalue the extent of damages or losses, resulting in lower payouts to victims.
4. **Misrepresentation of policy terms**: Insurance companies may misrepresent policy terms or conditions, making it difficult for victims to understand their rights and obligations.
5. **Failure to investigate**: Insurance companies may fail to investigate claims thoroughly, resulting in a lack of evidence to support victims' claims.
