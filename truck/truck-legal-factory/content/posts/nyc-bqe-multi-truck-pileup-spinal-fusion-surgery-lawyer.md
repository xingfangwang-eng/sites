6. **Police investigation report**: Obtain a copy of the police investigation report, which will detail the findings of the investigation and any evidence collected.

9. **Bills and receipts**: Gather bills and receipts related to medical expenses, including hospital bills, doctor's fees, and medication costs.

10. **Expert testimony**: Consider hiring experts, including medical professionals and engineers, to provide testimony and evidence to support your claims.
